Frank is an experienced design leader and product manager with expertise in strategic thinking. With 15+ years of experience, he has worked on various technologies, from mobile devices to HoloLens. His interdisciplinary background in design and business allows him to create products that are visually appealing and deliver measurable results.

This feature integrates with the Windows operating system to help users stay focused and take regular breaks, resulting in increased productivity and positive health outcomes. The feature has received widespread acclaim from users, industry experts, and media outlets.

In his free time, Frank enjoys spending time with his children, drawing, reading, and immersing himself in nature.
