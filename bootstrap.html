Frank is a seasoned design leader, product manager, design strategist with 15+ years of experience working at the forefront of technology from mobile devices to HoloLens. As a product manager and design leader, strategic thinking is one of his core strengths. He's constantly looking at the big picture, understanding market trends, and identifying opportunities for growth and improvement. His background in both design and business allows him to bridge the gap between form and function, ensuring that every product he works on not only looks great but also delivers measurable results.

He has a proven track record of delivering successful products and designs that meet or exceed user expectations. For example, he recognized that many users struggle with distractions and procrastination when working from home, leading to decreased productivity and negative health outcomes. To address this problem, he incubated and led the development the Focus Sessions feature that integrates with the Windows operating system to help users focus on their work and take breaks at regular intervals.

The result was a highly successful product that has received rave reviews from users and industry experts alike. Users report increased productivity and better health outcomes, and the tool has been featured in numerous media outlets and industry conferences.

He enjoys spending his spare time with his family, drawing, reading, and being in the nature.
